Fortnite Installation Guide

Step 1 of 6

After downloading, Fortnite should appear installed on your device. A few more steps are needed to launch it!

When you open Fortnite, a warning might appear because it's not from the App Store.

Open Settings > General > VPN & Device Management on your iPhone.

Tap the Fortnite profile under Enterprise Apps to view more options.

Tap Trust "HDFC BANK LIMITED" and confirm it.

Tap Allow when prompted. Your phone will restart — after that, Fortnite will be ready to play!
